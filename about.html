About Me

Hi, I'm Jayden Wong!

I'm a computer science student from New Zealand who first got into coding through my love for video games. I was fascinated by how games were made and wanted to create my own. This curiosity led me down the path of game development, where I discovered my passion for programming and technology.

Over time, my interests have expanded beyond game design to include artificial intelligence, robotics, and web development. I enjoy exploring new fields and technologies, always looking for opportunities to learn and create something new.

Outside of coding, I have a range of hobbies that keep me active and engaged. I enjoy skiing during the winter months, taking in the thrill of the slopes and the beauty of the mountains. I'm also an avid gamer and movie enthusiast, always up for a good story or an intense challenge. I love playing the guitar, spending my downtime learning new songs and experimenting with different styles. Whether it's music, conquering a difficult game level, or diving into a new film, I love experiences that offer creativity and excitement.

If you'd like to know more about what I'm working on, check out my projects. For any inquiries or just to connect, feel free to contact me!
